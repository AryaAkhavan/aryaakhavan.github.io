Arya Akhavan

I am currently a postdoctoral research associate at the University of Oxford. Previously, I spent a year as a postdoc at CMAP, École Polytechnique, Paris. I am interested in optimization in all aspects and mainly its applications in online learning. I did my PhD at Crest, ENSAE, Institut Polytechnique de Paris, and Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT), Genova, where I was fortunate to be advised by Alexandre B. Tsybakov and Massimiliano Pontil. I defended my thesis on February 3, 2023. You can find here my PhD thesis.
